Raythos Aircraft



View Source Code

The Raythos Aerospace Online System project aims to digitize aircraft manufacturing and delivery, focusing on user-friendly interactions with secure registration and customization options. It features a comprehensive aircraft catalog, streamlined ordering processes, and real-time manufacturing lifecycle monitoring. The project also includes efficient inventory control and coordinated transportation logistics, ensuring a seamless and secure customer experience from browsing to delivery. This initiative represents Raythos Aerospace's commitment to integrating technology for improved efficiency and customer satisfaction in the aerospace industry.

Used Spiral Methodology To Develop

Used Adobe XD for Desgining and Prototype, Developed using Visual studio . Testing White Box & Black Box & Automated (Selenium). Used ASP.NET MVC framework and the concepts of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)

User Requirements

  • Customer: The customer engages with the Raythos Aerospace system to explore and interact with the available aircraft models. They can register and log into their accounts, browse the detailed Aircraft Catalog with specifications and pricing, and customize their desired aircraft. They can select customization options and making secure online payments. Customers can also track the manufacturing progress of their ordered aircraft, receive real-time updates, and access estimated delivery dates
  • Admin: The admin user in the Raythos Aerospace system plays a pivotal role in overseeing and managing the entire online platform with access to an Admin Dashboard to handle various administrative tasks. Such as managing user accounts, manage customer data, monitoring and tracking orders, updating inventory, and generating reports. The admin user acts as the backbone of the system, ensuring smooth operations, security, and informed decision-making.
  • Control-Assistant (CA): He is the person who manage all the order stages. Order placed to Order Delivery
